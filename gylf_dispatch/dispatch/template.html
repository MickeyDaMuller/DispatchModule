Warm greetings in the precious and matchless name of our Lord and Saviour, Jesus Christ.

This is still our month of Prayer. Ephesians 6:18 says, "Praying always with all prayer and supplications in the Spirit, and watching thereunto with all perseverance and supplication for all saints."

Prayer is a privilege and an honour as it is an investment for eternity. When we pray, we are fellowshipping with the Father and His Son, Jesus Christ. Why should we pray?

a) Prayer seasons and enriches our spirits as we get connected to the Lord's ideas, visions and leading more easily in order to change the world.
b) As we pray we make tremendous power available, causing changes in our favour. It enables us to change circumstances.
c) Prayer provides a rich communion with the Lord.
